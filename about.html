About Us

Established in 2015 in Jaipur, Rajasthan Tavel is an independent travel agency specialising in organising tailor-made travel experiences for our guests. We are proud to say that we are a truly, professionally-managed travel agency, under the management of proprietor, Mr. Raja Khan & Mr. Azar Khan.We are committed to providing all types of travel services, including bus and train reservations, taxi services, hotel bookings and internal flights, as well as providing customised tour packages. A few of our guests already know when they contact us, where they would like to stay, however for the most part we provide hotel/accommodation recommendations and bookings throughout Rajasthan, from budget to top end hotels including various heritage properties. Our designed tailor-made itineraries are put together according to your plan and budget. So just browse the best tourist destinations in Rajasthan and book yourself a car and driver, plus hotels and hostels and get ready for a trip of a lifetime; to the land of Palaces & Forts, Deserts, Oasis, Camels, Wild-life sanctuaries and so much more.
